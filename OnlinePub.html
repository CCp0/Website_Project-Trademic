Online Pub

The Online Pub is where musicians can have a session online.

How this works is when the person calls a tune, their audio plays and after someone joining in plays a bar of music to the rhythm of the leading musician, the program accounts for the delay and merges the sounds together.
People can join in and listen at any point, however, some rooms may be private ( ) which means you can listen but cannot play along without prior permissions
