Tin Whistle

The tin whistle is easy to learn but hard to master. This instrument is widely played across Ireland and taught in most national schools early in the life of Irish students but too few continue to hone their skills thereafter. In the inexperienced hands this instrument can break pitch very easily, tone, dexterity and steady airflow can all be difficult to balance.
